Skincare Routine FAQ

What is a basic skincare routine?

A basic skincare routine includes cleansing, toning, moisturizing, and applying sunscreen. This helps maintain healthy skin and prevent damage.

How often should I exfoliate?

Exfoliate 1-2 times a week to remove dead skin cells. Over-exfoliating can lead to irritation and sensitivity.

Can I use the same products for day and night?

It's recommended to use a lighter moisturizer and sunscreen during the day, and a richer moisturizer at night to support skin repair.

Should I use products with SPF daily?

Yes, applying SPF daily protects your skin from harmful UV rays, preventing premature aging and skin cancer.
