Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is your return policy?

Our return policy allows you to return items within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in their original condition with all tags attached. Please contact our customer service to initiate a return.

How do I order online?

Get into our website and go to contact us page then order from there .

Account & Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Some payment methods may not be available in all countries.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your payment information. We don't store your full credit card details on our servers. All transactions are processed through PCI-compliant payment processors.

Technical Support

The website isn't loading properly. What should I do?

First, try refreshing the page. If that doesn't work, clear your browser cache and cookies. If the problem persists, try using a different browser or device. You can also contact our support team with details about the issue.

How do I update my account information?

Log in to your account and go to the "Account Settings" page. From there you can update your personal information, email address, password, and notification preferences.

Why am I not receiving email notifications?

First, check your spam/junk folder. If our emails are there, mark them as "Not Spam". Also, ensure your email address in our system is correct and that you haven't unsubscribed from notifications. You can manage your email preferences in your account settings.
